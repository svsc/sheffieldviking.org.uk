The SVSC annual membership fees are collected when you join the club and then in February in successive years.

If you join in the later part of the season (August onwards), your membership fee will include the following season.

Payment should be by cash or cheque, by arrangement with the membership secretary. A membership form is available at the club, or here (PDF)
